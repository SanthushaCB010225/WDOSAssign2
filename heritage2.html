Adam's Peak

Most prestigous heritage that Sri Lanka has is the Adam's Peak, which is 7,360 feet high and located in the hill counrty of Sri Lanka. The place is venerated by Buddhists, Muslims and Hindus, which regard it as the footprint of the Buddha, Adam and Siva.

Adam's Peak is surrounded by forested hills which is a housing for varying from elephants to leopards including endemic species. Also Adam's Peak yield precious emeralds, rubies and sapphires. To visit Adam's Peak there's specific season for travel. From December to May is the right time to visit to it unless off season is hard to travel with the climate.
