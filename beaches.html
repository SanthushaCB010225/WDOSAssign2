Mirissa Beach

Mirissa on the south coast and right on the southern tip off the island. It's about a 2 hour drive from Katunayaka airport. Mirissa is a small beach town it's on a little hill that rises from the beach. A small town, large beach with backed grove if palm tress gives the beauty of nature. Arrow-shaped, with the arrow tip sticking natural jetty, calm good for swimming. Mirissa is also a great place for whale watching best in February and March.

Nilaveli Beach

A broad, sandy beach lined with palm trees and dotted with fishermen's boats. Nilaveli is situated in the North-coast of Sri Lanka, which is 30 min drive form the city of Trincomalee. This is popular with both local and tourists due to its amazing view and calmness. There are lots of little restaurants along the beach with most specializing in seafood.

Arugam bay Beach

Arugam bay is the best international surf beaches situated in Sri Lanka. With bright sand and deep blue water, beauty comes. This beach is a attraction for many tourists around the world and local. It is bit like a Hawaiian beach with water slopses down and a dry sandy section.
