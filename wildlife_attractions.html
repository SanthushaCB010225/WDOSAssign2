Sinharaja Rain Forest

This is a UNESCO-listed world heritage site where it is the last viable area of primary tropical rainforest in Sri Lanka. Featuring waterfalls, bubbling water pools, emerald fern..etc. Sinharaja is situated in the south west of Sri Lanka.

Hikkaduwa Marine National Park

The Hikkaduwa Marine National Park is in the southern province and is one of only 2 marine national parks in Sri Lanka. It is declared as a national reserve in 1988 and then developed into a national park in 2002. This is the home to many coral reefs. This is the most diverse and accessible offer some of the Sri Lanka's best diving opportunities.

Horton Plains

The Horton Plains measure upto 2100 meters above sea level. It is the highest plateau in Sri Lanka(also known as 'Roof of the island'). The environment of Horton plains is comprise largely of cloudly forset and mountain greenlands. Also have rich endemic plants and animals that adapted to the cool climate.
